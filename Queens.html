A tour through the best coffee shops in Queens

A BRIEF HISTORY

With a large amount of space in comparison to New York City, Queens has emerged as a coffee hot spot for many coffee fanatics. The area features a diverse set of local coffee brands with a very strong coffee presence in Long Island City. Particularly interesting about Queens is how unique each coffee shop is. For instance, Sweetleaf in queens creates its cold brew with chicory and mayple syrup.

GREY Coffee is a stylish cafe located in Queens. In addittion to coffee, this store sells ACAI bowls wihtin a larger food menu.

Sweetleaf Coffee Roasters in Queens is loved by many of the locals. This store's signature coffee items is called rocket fuel which comes in incredibly sleek packaging.
