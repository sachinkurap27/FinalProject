A tour through Manhattan's Coffee Shops


Open from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM every day of this week, Corvo coffee is dedicated to the consumer. This coffee shop is in a great location, located at 42nd and 9th and also ofers tea.

Characterized by a open seating environment and rustic feel, Birch Coffee serves up quality beverages. This coffee shop offers a brew guide to new users on its website.

Culture Espresso is an independent and locally owned coffee shop that opened in 2009. This shop also offers a daily assortment of fresh baked goods and am award Winning Chocolate Chip Cookie.

Paper is a neighborhood coffee shop whose seeks to deliver the highest quality coffee. This coffee shop is actually located inside a hotel and offers a large assortment of tea and baked goods.

Ralph's coffee utlizes Ralph's special blends, including Ralph's Roast, Decaf,and Espresso. Much of the produce featured is organically grown beans from Central America, South America, and Africa.

This shop is located in the Ace Hotel in Midtown Manhattan. Stumptown Ace opened in 2009 and has a service where it delivers coffee to customers.

Think Coffee is an incredible cafe that also focuses on social impact. The shop has several social initiatives and helps provide access to clean water through the sale of its coffee.

Urban Backayrd employs a modernistic look with a vintage feel. The coffee shop sells travel mugs and bamboo utensil sets in addition to coffee.

Founded in of 2020, Blank Street arose from the idea that excellent coffee should not come at a premium. This shop has a small format design focused on sustainability.
