A tour through Brooklyn's Best Coffee Shops


BROOKLYN SHOPS

Known as the Black Star Bakery & Cafe, this shop seeks to provide not only a great coffee experience, but a food experience. This coffee shop serves avocado toast, breakfeast toast, and waffles in addition to coffee.

Coca Grinder is an up and coming cafe. Unlike most coffee shops, this coffee shops roasts its own coffee to ensure quality and flavor. This control over the roasting proccess enables the shop to deliver a deeper roast.

Cup of Joe Coffee Company is a "vintage-inspired, boutique coffee shop that offers the independent style" and a neighborhood feel. The signature coffee item at this store is called cup of joe which includes a house blend and has an aroma of chicory.

Partners Coffee is an incredibly innovative coffee merchant. This coffee is sourced sustainably through long-term partnerships. Futhermore, the company has a very strong ecommerce prescence with many of its offerings shared through online.

PEP Bakehouse and Coffee Shop has operated since 2016. The cafe not only offers drinks liek coffee, but offers great treats like cookies and pasteries. The store also offers desserts like pies and quiches.

This coffee shop offers over 300 types of bakery goods made fresh each day. The cafe is also well known for its milk cream and mango cakes.

Variety Coffee Roasters is favored by many locals. This coffee shop packages and sells its own coffee beans to customers.

Callaghan Coffee Shop in Brooklyn has operated for years. This coffee shop also operates as a restaurant if customers are looking for a bite.

Sey Coffee is a Cafe in Brooklyn, serving coffees & pastries in a plant-filled space with a skylight.
